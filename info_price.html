Volume discounts
The prices shown on the price list and on this website are at our highest price level (the price you would pay for orders of 1-23 pieces). Below is the table of volume discounts we offer. Please note that these discounts do not apply to hats, aprons, neckwear or accessories. The discount level applies to the total number of pieces you order for any given style number (you may use different colors of the same style number to achieve quantity).
Level 1:Orders of 24-72 pieces per style - 2%
Level 2:Orders of 73-120 pieces per style - 4%
Level 3:Orders of 121-240 pieces per style - 5%
Level 4:Orders above 240 pieces - Please contact us for special pricing


Note: Discount quantities are calculated by style number. For example short & long sleeve versions of the same shirt have different style numbers so you may not combine styles to achieve quantity. You may combine different colors to achieve quantity.
