Our Brands

One of our greatest strengths is a product line which is unparalleled in industry. Our goal is to be a true 'single source' for all your needs. We bring together all the most popular brands in our industry, representing the latest in fashion, quality and value.


We pay close attention to industry trends and go the extra mile to make sure we have the most up-to-date styles, colors & designs. We have sought out premier manufacturers who offer styles specifically designed for women in colors, cuts & fabrics that you expect to find from a retail clothing supplier. In fact, many of the products we carry are sold in retail stores in malls and in department stores throughout the country. The difference is, you are paying significantly less purchasing from us and you get the benefit our our knowledge about which products will work best for your needs.
